# 01 · Constitution
*The soul document. When any file, spec, or instinct conflicts with this one, this one wins.*

## Prime directive
**Progress = validated learning, not completed checkboxes.** Founder's Quest exists to make founders fall in love with the problem, not their solution. The founder is the hero of the *quest*; the customer is the hero of the *story*; the app is the mentor — and a mentor's love is measured by the questions it refuses to let you skip.

## Question design laws
1. Past stories over future opinions (the Mom Test). Ask what someone *did*, never what they *would do*.
2. One clause per question. Depth comes from the ladder (clarify → probe assumption → demand evidence → invert viewpoint → trace implication), not from compounds.
3. Typed answers: a name, a number, a story, a list, a verbatim quote, an IF-THEN, or a decision — declared as a hint under each input.
4. No self-graded yes/no a founder in love can flatter themselves on.
5. Every stage ends in falsification or a sealed kill criterion.
6. Plain language in the ask; myth in the chrome (stage names, symbols, gates). A question must never need decoding.
7. Evidence demands, not word counts.
8. Reward disconfirmation — killed assumptions make the map truer.
9. Demanding but kind. Second person, warm, direct. Never shame; never flatter.
10. Capture before hardening: recording a raw thought (hunch, solution idea) costs two taps and zero justification. Justification comes later, as a test.

## System laws
- **Evidence tiers:** E0 Hunch · E1 Heard · E2 Said · E3 Seen · E4 Paid (mythic skin: Whisper/Rumor/Word/Deed/Gold). Only E2+ moves Truth. Tier codes never translate; tier names may.
- **Dual progress:** Truth (importance-weighted assumptions resolved on E2+ evidence) leads; Action (milestones, self-reported) follows. High Action + low Truth summons the Shadow — except in the trough.
- **Invalidation pays 1.5×** validation. Guardians' evidence tiers are *derived* from linked ledger entries, never self-declared.
- **Gates warn, never block.** Overrides require a written reason, are logged to the trail, and appear in exports. Autonomy preserved; self-deception made visible.
- **The Vault:** solution ideas in Act I are captured, visible, and sealed until Stage 3.
- **Ariadne's Thread:** kill criteria are sealed and timestamped *before* results exist; the verdict is recorded before interpretation.
- **One-way feedback (PIE):** after a reading, name one change before the rebuttal box opens. Respond to feedback and the mind files it as fixed.
- **Cadence (PIE):** pressure belongs on the upswing, never in the trough. The Shadow holds fire when the Weather Trail reads rain-or-worse.
- **Joy is load-bearing.** The celebration question survives every rewrite; Side Quests are designed serendipity.
- **The human-contact test:** every feature must increase contact with real humans — interviews, mentors, peers, dinners — never simulate it. If it replaces contact, it's out.

## The floor (required in anything calling itself Founder's Quest, including editions)
Evidence tiers · falsification and sealed kill criteria · honest-not-nice with the decision belonging to the founder · the human-contact test · nothing that could target, exploit, or endanger people (T&S review on recipes touching vulnerable users, data collection, or guardrails).

## Voice & copy
Plain verbs, sentence case, active voice. Errors say what happened and what to do. No jargon in asks, no marketing anywhere, no hedging rituals, no "learnings." Data minimization is stated as a design choice. Never-translate invariants: `E0–E4`, "Founder's Quest," "Genesis Framework."
